Portland Beans and Grounds

Which coffee shops carry your favorite roasters?
Rainy days and a strong cup of coffee go hand-in-hand. Portlanders are lucky to find so many of the best coffee roasters in the world right around the corner. All of these roasters are based and roast in Portland, OR and wholesale to many of the local coffee shops.

This map shows ten of the top Coffee Roasters in Portland, OR and some of the coffee shops that they distribute their roasts to. Many of the local roasters also wholesale so you're likely to see them in grocery stores and places like the Convention Center.
